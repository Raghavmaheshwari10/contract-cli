VENDOR SUPPLY AGREEMENT

Agreement Date: March 1, 2026

BETWEEN:

BUYER: TechVision Solutions Pvt. Ltd. ("Company")
       42 MG Road, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560001

AND

VENDOR: Globex Office Supplies Ltd. ("Vendor")
        12 Industrial Area, Phase II, Noida, UP 201301

RECITALS:
The Company wishes to engage the Vendor for the supply of office furniture,
IT equipment, and consumables for its Bengaluru and Hyderabad offices.

1. PRODUCTS AND SERVICES
   The Vendor shall supply:
   a) Office furniture (desks, chairs, storage units)
   b) IT peripherals (monitors, keyboards, mice, headsets)
   c) Office consumables (paper, toner, stationery)
   d) Installation and assembly services for all furniture

2. CONTRACT DURATION
   Start Date: April 1, 2026
   End Date: March 31, 2027 (12 months)
   Renewal: Auto-renews for successive 12-month periods unless either
   party gives 60 days written notice before expiry.

3. PRICING AND PAYMENT
   a) Pricing: As per the attached Schedule A (Rate Card)
   b) Estimated Annual Value: INR 45,00,000 (Forty-Five Lakhs)
   c) Payment Terms: Net 45 days from delivery and acceptance
   d) Early Payment Discount: 2% discount for payment within 15 days
   e) All prices are exclusive of GST (18% applicable)

4. DELIVERY TERMS
   a) Standard delivery: Within 7 business days of purchase order
   b) Bulk orders (above INR 5,00,000): Within 15 business days
   c) Emergency orders: Best effort within 48 hours (15% surcharge applies)
   d) Delivery Location: Company premises in Bengaluru and Hyderabad
   e) Free delivery for orders above INR 25,000

5. WARRANTY
   a) Furniture: 5-year warranty against manufacturing defects
   b) IT Equipment: 3-year warranty or manufacturer warranty (whichever is longer)
   c) Consumables: Replacement for defective items within 7 days

6. QUALITY STANDARDS
   a) All products must meet BIS (Bureau of Indian Standards) certification
   b) IT equipment must be energy-star rated
   c) Vendor shall provide test/compliance certificates upon request

7. PENALTIES
   a) Late Delivery: 1% of order value per day, maximum 10%
   b) Defective Products: Full replacement at vendor's cost + 5% penalty
   c) Repeated quality failures (3 or more in a quarter): Company may
      terminate with 15 days notice

8. INSURANCE
   Vendor shall maintain product liability insurance of INR 1,00,00,000
   (One Crore) for the duration of this agreement.

9. TERMINATION
   a) For convenience: 60 days written notice
   b) For cause: 15 days notice after failure to cure within 30 days
   c) Immediate termination for fraud, bankruptcy, or insolvency

10. CONFIDENTIALITY
    Vendor shall not disclose Company's purchasing patterns, pricing
    negotiations, or internal requirements to any third party.

11. GOVERNING LAW
    This agreement is governed by the laws of India. Disputes shall be
    resolved in the courts of Bengaluru, Karnataka.
