SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 15, 2026,
by and between:

CLIENT: TechVision Solutions Pvt. Ltd. ("Company")
        Address: 42 MG Road, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560001

AND

SERVICE PROVIDER: Acme Cloud Services Inc. ("Provider")
        Address: 500 Market Street, San Francisco, CA 94105

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
   The Provider shall deliver the following cloud infrastructure services:
   a) Cloud hosting and server management (AWS-based)
   b) 24/7 system monitoring and incident response
   c) Monthly performance reports and optimization recommendations
   d) Data backup and disaster recovery services
   e) SSL certificate management and security patching

2. TERM
   This Agreement shall commence on February 1, 2026, and shall continue
   for a period of twenty-four (24) months, ending on January 31, 2028,
   unless terminated earlier in accordance with Section 8.

3. COMPENSATION
   a) Monthly Service Fee: USD 15,000 (Fifteen Thousand US Dollars)
   b) Payment Terms: Net 30 days from invoice date
   c) Annual escalation: 5% increase effective on each anniversary
   d) Total Contract Value: Approximately USD 378,000 over the full term
   e) Late Payment Penalty: 1.5% per month on overdue amounts

4. SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (SLA)
   a) Uptime Guarantee: 99.95% monthly uptime
   b) Response Time: Critical issues — 15 minutes; High — 1 hour; Medium — 4 hours
   c) SLA Credits:
      - Below 99.95% but above 99.9%: 10% credit on monthly fee
      - Below 99.9% but above 99.5%: 25% credit on monthly fee
      - Below 99.5%: 50% credit on monthly fee
   d) Maximum monthly SLA credit shall not exceed 50% of that month's fee

5. CONFIDENTIALITY
   Both parties agree to maintain strict confidentiality of all proprietary
   information, trade secrets, and business data shared during the term of
   this Agreement. This obligation survives termination for a period of
   three (3) years.

6. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
   All intellectual property developed specifically for the Company under
   this Agreement shall be owned by the Company. Pre-existing IP of the
   Provider remains with the Provider.

7. LIABILITY
   a) Provider's total liability shall not exceed the total fees paid in
      the twelve (12) months preceding the claim
   b) Neither party shall be liable for indirect, consequential, or
      punitive damages
   c) Provider maintains professional liability insurance of USD 5,000,000

8. TERMINATION
   a) Either party may terminate with 90 days written notice
   b) Immediate termination for material breach if not cured within 30 days
   c) Upon termination, Provider shall assist with data migration for up to
      60 days at no additional cost

9. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
   Any disputes shall be resolved through binding arbitration in
   Bengaluru, India, under the rules of the Indian Arbitration and
   Conciliation Act, 1996.

10. GOVERNING LAW
    This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of India.
